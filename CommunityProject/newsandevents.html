Little Joseph

After getting help from Little Children Organization, Little Joseph now lives happily with an american couple that adopted him and is taking really good care of him.

Amir

Amir was only a few months old when someone found him on the street and contacted us. Little Children Organization took him, raised him, and he recently got adopted by a single loving mother and lives in Canada.

Kadir Family

We found the Kadir kids a few months ago when an anonymous person contacted us about them. They lost there parents in a war and the big siblings were working to take care of their small siblings. Family Care took them, enrolled them into a school and now they have a nice house and are getting a good education.

ACT Facilitator Training- April 13 & 14, 2020 Langhorne

Oxford Valley Mall

2300 Lincoln Highway, Unit 12, Langhorne, PA 19047

ACT Raising Safe Kids is an evidence-based parenting curriculum developed and managed by the APA Office of Violence Prevention. This program teaches counselors, social workers, nurses, teachers, child welfare workers, and parent educators how to facilitate the nine-week parenting program for caregivers of children ages 0-10. The program is easy to implement, includes ongoing technical assistance, coaching, and a plethora of program related resources.
