Who we are

We are an organisation working with other organizations all over the world to protect and help children.

How to help the children you see

By contacting us, you could be helping a child or more to grow in a healthier environment.
If you see a homeless child, an orphan, or a child being abused, go to our Contact page and fill in the information accurately about the children you see.
You do not have to provide your name when filling the information, we respect your privacy and we do not need to know any private information about you.
You can also help by donating. 90% of your donations go to the organizations we work with, and the rest helps us find more children.
To stay updated about the children we help, you can go to our News and Events page and to keep track of the children you contact us about, you can enter your name and email, and enable email notifications to get notified whenever we make progress with the case you submitted or if we have any upcoming events you might be interested in.

How it works

When we recieve your messages about the children, we filter them using the information you entered, that is by location, age, and state.
After filtering, we choose the most proper organization that we work with and send them the information you sent us.
Then, this organization finds the children and helps them and keeps us updated about how they're doing and where they are.

What our goal is

Our goal is to help make this world a better place by saving children from their unsafe environments.
